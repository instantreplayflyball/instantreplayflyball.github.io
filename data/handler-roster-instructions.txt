INSTANT REPLAY FLYBALL — ROSTER & PHOTOS FOR THE NEW WEBSITE
================================================================================

We are rebuilding the team page. Please add your information in TWO places:
(1) the roster spreadsheet, and (2) the shared photos folder. Both links are at
the bottom of this message.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 1 — ROSTER SPREADSHEET (ONE ROW PER DOG)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

• Do not change or delete the header row (row 1: order, handler, dog, breed,
  bio, photo, photoAlt).

• Add ONE new row per dog you are listing.

• Columns — what to enter:

  order      Optional. Leave blank unless you are asked to use a specific
             number. (We can sort the list later.)

  handler    Your name (or names) exactly as you want it shown on the website.

  dog        Your dog’s call name — spelling and capitalization as you want it
             on the site.

  breed      Optional. Examples: Border Collie, Whippet. Leave blank if unsure.

  bio        A short bio (a few sentences). For a second paragraph, press
             Option+Enter (Mac) or Alt+Enter (Windows) INSIDE the same cell —
             do not start a new row for paragraph two. (A new row = a different
             dog.)

  photo      Leave this BLANK for now, OR type only a photo filename you will
             use in Part 2, for example:  dog-marley.jpg
             Do not paste long sentences here — only a filename or blank.
             (No need to type the full path; we use images/dogs/ on our side.)

  photoAlt   Optional but appreciated: one line describing the photo for people
             who use screen readers, e.g. “Border collie MARLEY at the flyball
             box.” If you leave it blank, we will use a simple default.

• Do not put photos inside the spreadsheet cells. The site cannot use images
  embedded in the sheet — use the shared folder in Part 2.

• If you handle more than one dog, add one row per dog (you may repeat your
  handler name on each row).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 2 — DOG PHOTOS (GOOGLE DRIVE FOLDER)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

• Upload ONE clear photo per dog to the shared “images” folder (link below).

• Good lighting and a roughly square or 4×3 picture work well.

• Name the file so we can match it to your dog, for example:
    dog-CALLNAME.jpg
  Use your dog’s call name, lowercase, with hyphens instead of spaces if
  needed (e.g. dog-peach-juice.jpg).

• If you entered a filename in the spreadsheet “photo” column, use that EXACT
  name for the file you upload.

• Accepted types: .jpg or .jpeg are ideal; .png is OK. Avoid HEIC if possible
  (export as JPG from your phone before uploading).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHEN YOU ARE DONE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

• Double-check your row: handler, dog, and bio filled in; breed and photoAlt
  if you have them; “photo” is either blank or matches your uploaded filename.

• You do not need to export or “publish” anything — the web team will pull the
  sheet and photos when we update the site.
